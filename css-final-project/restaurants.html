Info

Eagle River has to offer some fantastic restaraunts, many of them being northwoods themed. Many restaraunts offer a specialty Friday Fish Fry, like Kickback Bar and Grill and Eagle Waters Resort Restaraunt. There are taverns that offer unique and delectable creations, like The Craftsman American Tavern or Eddie B's White Spruce Restaraunt and Tavern. If you're looking to feed your sweet tooth, there are many places for tasty treets like Pirates's Hideaway or Eagle Baking Company.s
